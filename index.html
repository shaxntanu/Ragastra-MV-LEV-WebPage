About the Project

Team Raga MV LevTeam Raga is a student-led initiative focused on developing a sustainable and futuristic Levitating Campus Mobility Vehicle. The project is inspired by the principles of magnetic levitation, specifically Electromagnetic Suspension (EMS), and is envisioned as a compact, eco-friendly solution tailored for university campuses.

By combining advanced engineering concepts with a practical mini-metro sized design, Team Raga aims to revolutionize short-distance transportation within academic and institutional spaces.

The Concept

The vehicle operates on the fundamental principle of Electromagnetic Suspension (EMS), where electromagnets generate lift and stability above a steel-based track. Unlike large-scale maglev trains designed for intercity travel, our design focuses on a miniaturized metro model optimized for the compact nature of campuses.

This ensures a safe, efficient, and silent transport experience while highlighting the practical applications of maglev technology in smaller, controlled environments.

The Vision Behind It

The vision of Team Raga is to create a mobility system that embodies innovation, sustainability, and accessibility. We aspire to redefine how students and faculty commute across campus by replacing conventional ground-based vehicles with a levitation-based transport solution that is energy-efficient, low-maintenance, and futuristic.

This initiative reflects our long-term commitment to advancing modern engineering practices while addressing pressing mobility challenges within educational ecosystems.

Testing Ground

The campus serves as the perfect testing ground for futuristic transportation systems. By implementing EMS-based levitation technology in this context, Team Raga demonstrates how advanced concepts can be scaled down to meet real-world needs.

Educational Platform

The project not only provides a reliable mode of transport but also acts as an educational platform—encouraging research, innovation, and interdisciplinary collaboration among engineering students.

Our Commitment

Team Raga is committed to precision engineering, sustainable practices, and impactful innovation. Our project represents more than just a transport solution; it is a statement about the future of mobility.

With continued research, collaboration, and institutional support, we aim to transform our concept into a fully functional system that inspires both academic and technological progress.
